OUR STORY

About THE DARK WOOD Interiors & Decorators

We are a distinguished partnership firm based in Bharatpur, Rajasthan, dedicated to transforming spaces through exceptional woodcraft and contemporary interior solutions.

With years of expertise in precision wood CNC cutting, CNC door craftsmanship, and bespoke design, we have established ourselves as the preferred choice for discerning clients across Rajasthan. Our comprehensive portfolio spans designer modern and traditional wooden doors, CNC-crafted custom woodwork, bespoke furniture manufacturing, and complete contract-based interior solutions.

At THE DARK WOOD, we believe that every space tells a story. Our commitment to durability, finishing excellence, and competitive pricing ensures that your vision is realized without compromise. From individual pieces to full-site interior contracts, we deliver craftsmanship that stands the test of time.

Founded by Mohd. Akram · Devesh Kumar · Rohit Bansal
